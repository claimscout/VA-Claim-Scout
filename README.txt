VA CLAIM SCOUT -- Claims Intelligence
================================================================================
Free. Completely free. For every veteran. Always.
================================================================================

VA Claim Scout is an AI research framework that helps U.S. veterans analyze
their own medical and service records to identify potential VA disability
claims -- primary, presumptive, secondary, TDIU, and SMC.

It is not a product. It is not a service. It does not cost anything.
It is a free tool built by a veteran, for veterans.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT IT DOES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

VA Claim Scout turns any capable AI platform into a structured claims research
assistant. Upload your records, type one command, and the AI walks you through:

  - Identifying potential new and secondary claims
  - Scanning for denial risks in your own records
  - Finding interconnected conditions (the Kinetic Chain)
  - Building Tier 1 evidence through doctor scripts
  - Preparing for C&P exams with worst-day talking points
  - Drafting outreach to your VSO or attorney
  - Checking for TDIU and SMC eligibility
  - Understanding VA Math and effective dates

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW TO USE IT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

STEP 1 -- Get your records

Upload as many of these as you have:

  - VA Blue Button Report
    (VA.gov > My HealtheVet > Health Records > VA Blue Button > Select All
     > Download PDF)
  - DD-214 -- your military discharge paperwork
  - Rating Decision Letters -- any past VA claim decisions
  - Community Care or private doctor records
  - C&P exam results
  - Emails with your VSO or attorney
  - Claim denial letters or appeal filings

STEP 2 -- Choose an AI platform

VA Claim Scout works with:
  - Claude (claude.ai)             RECOMMENDED
  - ChatGPT (chatgpt.com)
  - Google NotebookLM (notebooklm.google.com)
  - Google Gemini (gemini.google.com)

STEP 3 -- Upload your files

  Claude & ChatGPT:  Click the paperclip icon and upload your records.
  NotebookLM:        Click "+ Add Source" and upload all files including
                     VA_Claim_Scout_v0.2.md

STEP 4 -- Type this and press Enter

  "Claim Scout, help."

VA Claim Scout will introduce itself, show you everything it can do, and ask
where you want to start. Just follow its lead.
