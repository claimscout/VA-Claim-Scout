# VA Claim Scout — Claims Intelligence

> **Free. Completely free. For every veteran. Always.**

It's a free AI-powered research tool that helps veterans dig through their own VA records and find potential disability claims they may have missed — secondary claims, medication side effects that qualify as new claims, denial risks hiding in their own records, and more. It's not a lawyer. It's not official. But it's the kind of research assistant I wish I'd had.

**Current Version: v0.3 — March 2026**
**GitHub:** github.com/ClaimScout

---

## What It Is and Isn't

- **It is completely free.** No cost, no subscription, no catch — ever.
- **It is private and secure.** You control your data. Your records go directly to the AI platform you choose — nothing is stored by me or anyone else.
- **It is simple.** The bulk of the project is a single file, and it is open source. Anyone can read exactly how it works.
- **It does not file claims for you.** It helps you find and prepare them.
- **It works on Claude, ChatGPT, Google NotebookLM, and others.**
- **A VSO or VA-accredited attorney should always review findings before you file anything.**

---

## What It Does

VA Claim Scout turns any capable AI platform into a structured claims research assistant. Upload your records, type one command, and the AI walks you through:

- Identifying potential new and secondary claims
- Scanning for denial risks in your own records
- Finding interconnected conditions (the Kinetic Chain)
- Building Tier 1 evidence through doctor scripts
- Preparing for C&P exams with worst-day talking points
- Writing buddy statements — guided, legally valid lay evidence
- Writing impact statements — work, social, family, and sexual functioning
- Drafting outreach to your VSO or attorney
- Checking for TDIU and SMC eligibility
- Understanding VA Math and effective dates

## How to Use It — 4 Steps

**Step 1 — Get Your Records**

The more you upload, the better. Start with your VA Blue Button Report — it is the most important file.

> VA.gov → My HealtheVet → Health Records → VA Blue Button → Select All → Download PDF
> Direct link: https://www.va.gov/my-health/medical-records/download

If you have a DD-214, rating decision letters, C&P exam results, or private doctor records, upload those too.

**Step 2 — Choose an AI Platform**

- [Claude](https://claude.ai) *(recommended — free to start)*
- [ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com)
- [Google NotebookLM](https://notebooklm.google.com)
- [Google Gemini](https://gemini.google.com)

Create a free account if you don't have one.

**Step 3 — Upload Your Files**

Click the paperclip icon in the chat and upload everything you gathered. Also upload `VA_Claim_Scout_v0.3.md` — this is the framework file that tells the AI how to analyze your records.

**Step 4 — Type This and Press Enter**

```
Claim Scout, help.
```

That's it. The AI will introduce itself, show you what it can do, and guide you from there. You can ask follow-up questions in plain English the whole way through.
