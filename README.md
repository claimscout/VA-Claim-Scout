# VA Claim Scout — Claims Intelligence

> **Free. Completely free. For every veteran. Always.**

VA Claim Scout is an AI research framework that helps U.S. veterans analyze their own medical and service records to identify potential VA disability claims — primary, presumptive, secondary, TDIU, and SMC.

It is not a product. It is not a service. It does not cost anything.  
It is a free tool built by a veteran, for veterans.

---

## What It Does

VA Claim Scout turns any capable AI platform into a structured claims research assistant. Upload your records, type one command, and the AI walks you through:

- Identifying potential new and secondary claims
- Scanning for denial risks in your own records
- Finding interconnected conditions (the Kinetic Chain)
- Building Tier 1 evidence through doctor scripts
- Preparing for C&P exams with worst-day talking points
- Drafting outreach to your VSO or attorney
- Checking for TDIU and SMC eligibility
- Understanding VA Math and effective dates

---

## How to Use It

**Step 1 — Get your records**

Upload as many of these as you have:
- VA Blue Button Report *(VA.gov → My HealtheVet → Health Records → VA Blue Button → Select All → Download PDF)*
- DD-214 — your military discharge paperwork
- Rating Decision Letters — any past VA claim decisions
- Community Care or private doctor records
- C&P exam results
- Emails with your VSO or attorney
- Claim denial letters or appeal filings

**Step 2 — Choose an AI platform**

VA Claim Scout works with:
- [Claude](https://claude.ai) *(recommended)*
- [ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com)
- [Google NotebookLM](https://notebooklm.google.com)
- [Google Gemini](https://gemini.google.com)

**Step 3 — Upload your files**

On Claude or ChatGPT: click the paperclip icon and upload your records.  
On NotebookLM: click "+ Add Source" and upload all files including `VA_Claim_Scout_v0.2.md`.

**Step 4 — Type this and press Enter**

```
Claim Scout, help.
```

VA Claim Scout will introduce itself, show you everything it can do, and ask where you want to start. Just follow its lead.
